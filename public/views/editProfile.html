First Login

Welcome to the HIT Portal.

Please enter your current rank and pick a promotion tag. After editing the fields click the "Edit Profile" button and wait for a green alert confirming it.

Not updating this information correctly will prevent you from adding logs.

The promotion tag should be a short version (4 characters max) of your username without adding any brackets.

Whenever you are promoted, please update your rank in your profile. Forgetting to maintain it up to date may cause problems when your logs are later reviewed.

After successfully updating your rank and promotion tag, click the "Home" button on the left side (or top on smaller screens) and explore the HIT Portal.

See you in base!
